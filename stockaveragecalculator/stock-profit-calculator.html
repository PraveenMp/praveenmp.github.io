What is stock Profit or Loss Calculator?

Stock Profit or Loss Calculator is a tool that helps you calculate the profit or loss you made on your stock investments. It takes into account the number of stocks you own, the buying price per stock, the selling price per stock, and the number of stocks you want to sell. The calculator then calculates the total investment, total sold stocks, remaining shares, and the profit or loss you made on your stock investments.

  1. Enter the number of stocks you own in the "Number of Stocks" field.
  2. Enter the buying price per stock in the "Buying Price per Stock" field.
  3. Enter the selling price per stock in the "Selling Price per Stock" field.
  4. Enter the number of stocks you want to sell in the "Number of Stocks to Sell" field.
  5. Click on the "Calculate" button to calculate the profit or loss you made on your stock investments.
