Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) Calculator

A Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) calculator is a financial tool used to calculate the final value of an investment after a specified period and the total amount withdrawn through monthly withdrawals. It helps investors plan their withdrawals from an investment portfolio while ensuring that they maintain a desired income stream over time.

To use the SWP calculator, enter the initial investment amount, monthly withdrawal amount, annual interest rate, and number of months in the form below and click "Calculate". The calculator will then compute the final value after the specified number of months and the total amount withdrawn, and display the results.
