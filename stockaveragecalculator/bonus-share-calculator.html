Bonus Share Calculator
Use this calculator to determine the new stock price and the number of shares you will own after a bonus issue.
A bonus issue increases the number of shares in a company by issuing more shares to current shareholders based on their existing holding. In a 2:1 bonus issue, for every 1 share owned, you get 2 additional shares.
Inputs:
Current Stock Price (₹): The current price per share of the stock.
Bonus Ratio (Bonus:Held): The ratio indicating how many bonus shares will be given for each held share.
Number of Shares Owned: The number of shares you currently own.
Outputs:
New Stock Price: The adjusted price per share after the bonus issue.
Total Shares After Bonus: The total number of shares you will own after the bonus issue.
Additional Shares: The number of new shares received as bonus.
Investment Value: The total value of your investment should theoretically remain the same.
